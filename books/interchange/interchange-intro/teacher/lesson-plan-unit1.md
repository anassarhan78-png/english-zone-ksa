# Lesson Plan - Unit 1: It's nice to meet you (Level 1A)

## Week 1 (5 lectures + test)

### Before starting the level (First session intro - 30 mins)
- Introduce teacher and centre (7 min)
- Learn students' names (2 min)
- Talk about books, CD, workbook, language summary (3 min)
- Collect phone numbers and create WhatsApp group (5-7 min)
- Add students to Class Dojo for rewards, attendance (5-7 min)

---

## Lecture 1 (Sunday) - 1 hour

| Time | Activity | Details |
|------|----------|---------|
| 10 min | Vocabulary Quizlet | Introduce unit vocab (names, greetings) |
| 7 min | Dictation | 5 words (name, first, last, teacher, student) |
| 10 min | Conversation p.2 | Students close books, listen and repeat |
| 5 min | Workbook check | Homework review |
| 5 min | Revision | Previous vocabulary |
| 5 min | Listening p.3 ex5 | Numbers and names |
| 10 min | Page 4 - Titles | Explain Miss, Mrs., Ms., Mr. Use live worksheet |
| 8 min | Wrap-up & homework | Assign dictation words + workbook p.1 |

**Homework:** Page 1 ex1 + dictation (5 words English-Arabic)

---

## Lecture 2 (Monday) - 1 hour

| Time | Activity | Details |
|------|----------|---------|
| 10 min | Vocabulary Quizlet | Review + new words |
| 7 min | Dictation | 5 words (Miss, Mr., Mrs., Ms., book) |
| 10 min | Page 5+6 ex 9 A+B | Powerpoint: subject pronouns + verb be |
| 10 min | Grammar practice | Students substitute names in conversation |
| 10 min | Pair work | Role play greetings |
| 8 min | Listening ex 12 | Numbers and emails |
| 5 min | Homework | Page 2+3 + dictation titles (English-English) |

## Lecture 4 (Wednesday) - 1 hour

| Time | Activity | Details |
|------|----------|---------|
| 10 min | Vocabulary Quizlet | All unit words |

---

## Lecture 5 (Thursday) - 1 hour (Revision & Presentation)

| Time | Activity | Details |
|------|----------|---------|
| 15 min | Revision game | Wordwall or Quizlet live |
| 20 min | Presentations | Each student introduces themselves (30 sec) |
| 10 min | Dictation review | All 20+ words from week |
| 10 min | Q&A | Teacher asks questions (first name, last name, phone number) |
| 5 min | Prepare for test | Remind of format |

---

## Lecture 6 (Friday/Saturday) - FIRST TEST (1 hour)

- Written test: dictation (10 words), fill blanks, matching, short writing.
- Oral test (optional): introduce yourself.

---

## Follow-up & Weak Performance Plan
- Extra dictation practice for weak students.
- Pair strong with weak for role plays.
- Provide audio recordings of all vocabulary.
- Use Class Dojo to reward small improvements.
