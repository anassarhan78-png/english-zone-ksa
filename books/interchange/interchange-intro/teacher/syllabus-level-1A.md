# Syllabus - Interchange Intro Level 1A (Units 1-4)

## Course Overview
- **Duration:** 4 weeks (4 units) + final test
- **Schedule:** Sunday to Thursday (1 hour/day), test on 6th day
- **Target Audience:** Adult beginners (with Dyslexia support)
- **Materials:** Interchange Intro Student's Book, Workbook, CD, Language Summary

## Learning Objectives
By the end of Level 1A, students will be able to:
- Introduce themselves and others
- Use subject pronouns and verb "be" (am/is/are)
- Use possessive adjectives (my, your, his, her)
- Ask and answer about names, phone numbers, jobs
- Greet appropriately at different times of day
- Spell names and numbers 0-10
- Fill out a basic business card

## Weekly Breakdown

| Week | Unit | Topics | Test |
|------|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Unit 1 | Names, introductions, titles, verb be, numbers 0-10 | Friday |
| 2 | Unit 2 | Classroom objects, plurals, this/that, articles | Friday |
| 3 | Unit 3 | Family members, possessive 's, adjectives | Friday |
| 4 | Unit 4 | Daily routines, time, present simple | Friday |
| 5 | Review | Level 1A final exam | - |

## Assessment
- Weekly dictation (10%)
- Weekly quiz (20%)
- Class participation (10%)
- Homework (10%)
- Presentations (10%)
- Unit tests (40%)

## Dyslexia Support
- Use OpenDyslexic font in all digital materials.
- Provide audio for all written texts.
- Allow extra time for reading/writing tasks.
- Use multi-sensory activities (role play, songs, gestures).
- Avoid crowded slides; use high contrast colors.
