# Game Bank - Interchange Intro

## For Classroom (Teacher-led)

### 1. Name Chain
- First student says: "My name is [name]."
- Second: "His/Her name is [first student's name], and my name is [second's name]."
- Continue around class.

### 2. Spelling Bee
- Teacher says a word (e.g., "book").
- Students take turns spelling it aloud.
- If wrong, next student tries.

### 3. Role Play: Greetings
- Pairs act out hello/goodbye at different times (morning, afternoon, evening).

### 4. Business Card Bingo
- Students create fake business cards.
- Teacher reads details (e.g., "Phone number ends with 01").
- Students mark if they have that.

## For Online / Computer Lab

### 5. Wordwall Games
- Matching pairs (word + meaning)
- Quiz show
- Group sort (male/female titles)

### 6. Quizlet Live
- Team competition for vocabulary.

## For Dyslexia Support
- Use large buttons, clear fonts, audio support.
- Avoid time pressure (allow extra seconds).
- Use color coding (e.g., all nouns blue, all verbs green).
