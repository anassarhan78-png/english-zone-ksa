# Teacher's Rubric - Interchange Intro Level 1A

## For Each Student (Weekly)

| Criteria | Excellent (5) | Good (4) | Satisfactory (3) | Needs Work (2) | Poor (1) |
|----------|---------------|----------|------------------|----------------|----------|
| **Dictation** | 100% correct | 80-99% | 60-79% | 40-59% | <40% |
| **Quiz** | 90-100% | 75-89% | 60-74% | 45-59% | <45% |
| **Speaking/Presentation** | Fluent, few errors | Clear, some errors | Understandable | Hesitant | Cannot communicate |
| **Homework** | Completed fully | Mostly done | Partially done | Little effort | None |
| **Participation** | Volunteers often | Participates when called | Rarely participates | Reluctant | None |

## Class Dojo Points
- +5 for perfect dictation
- +3 for good quiz score
- +2 for volunteering
- -2 for missing homework

## Weak Performance Plan
- Identify struggling students after week 1.
- Provide extra 10 min after class (twice a week).
- Pair with peer tutor.
- Simplify dictation to 3 words/day.
- Use audio-only drills.
