Lesson Plan - Unit 6 (5 lectures + test)

Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour each). Test: Next Sunday (1 hour). No classes on Friday/Saturday.

Digital tools: Quizlet sets (vocabulary), Kahoot (review games), Wordwall (interactive activities).

DayDictation WordsWarm-up / Guided PracticeIndependent Practice (Digital & Pair)Wrap-up & HW
Sunday bike, bus, car, motorcycle, subway, train, taxi Transportation vocabulary with Quizlet flashcards. Teacher asks “How do you go to school?” Wordwall matching (transport ↔ picture). Students use Quizlet “Learn”. Pair work: “How do you go to work? – I take the bus.” Kahoot transport quiz. HW: Write 5 sentences “I go to ... by ...”.
Monday father, mother, brother, sister, son, daughter, husband, wife Family vocabulary via Quizlet. Teacher introduces “This is my mother.” Wordwall family tree drag & drop. Students describe their family in pairs: “My father is a doctor.” Kahoot family members. HW: Draw your family tree and label in English.
Tuesday house, apartment, school, work, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday 大家一起学习地点和星期词汇。教师使用 Quizlet 播放音频，学生跟读。解释“work”作为名词。 Wordwall “days of the week” ordering. Pair work: “What do you do on Monday? – I go to school.” Kahoot days & places. HW: Write your weekly schedule (Monday to Friday).
Wednesday Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday, drive, ride, take, walk, live Simple present Wh-questions with Quizlet. Teacher models “Where do you live? – I live in a house.” Wordwall question formation. Students ask each other “What time do you get up?” Kahoot simple present. HW: Write 5 questions to ask a classmate about their routine.
Thursday Review all transport, family, days, verbs Review with Kahoot (full unit). Teacher leads conversation “Nice car!” (p.??). Quizlet “Test” mode. Role‑play: “Where does your brother work? – He works in a hotel.” Final revision. HW: Prepare for Sunday’s test (simple present Wh-questions, object pronouns).
Friday No class (official weekend)
Saturday No class (official weekend)
Next Sunday FINAL TEST – Unit 6 (1 hour)
Dictation (10 words), fill in blanks (simple present Wh-questions), matching (transport ↔ verb), short writing (describe a family member’s routine), oral (ask & answer about routine).

Weak performance plan: Extra Quizlet practice, peer tutoring, audio recordings, Class Dojo rewards.
