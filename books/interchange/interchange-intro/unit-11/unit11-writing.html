✍️ Unit 11: What are you going to do? - Writing Task

📝 Describe your plans for a special occasion

Instructions: Write a paragraph of 100-150 words. Choose one: your birthday, a holiday (e.g., New Year's, Christmas, Eid), or a weekend. Include:

  • What you are going to do (use "be going to")
  • When it is (date or time expression)
  • Where you are going to go
  • Who you are going to be with
  • What you are going to eat or celebrate
📌 Example:
"I am going to celebrate my birthday next Saturday, June 15th. I am going to invite my best friends to my house. We are going to order pizza and bake a cake. My mom is going to decorate the living room with balloons. After dinner, we are going to play music and dance. I am not going to receive many gifts, but I am going to have a lot of fun."
