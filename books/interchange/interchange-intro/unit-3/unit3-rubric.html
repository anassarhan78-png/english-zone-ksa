Lesson Plan - Unit 3 (5 lectures + test)

Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour each). Test: Next Sunday (1 hour). No classes on Friday/Saturday.

Digital tools: Quizlet sets (vocabulary), Kahoot (review games), Wordwall (interactive activities).

📘 Note on numbers: Numbers 1‑10 are practiced as words (one, two, three...). For numbers 11‑100, students learn the written forms (e.g., eleven, twenty‑three, one hundred) using regular text.
DayDictation WordsWarm-up / Guided PracticeIndependent Practice (Digital & Pair)Wrap-up & HW
Sunday Japan, Korea, China, Mexico, Brazil Introduce countries with Quizlet flashcards. Teacher asks “Where are you from?” Models answers. Wordwall matching (country ↔ flag). Pairs ask “Where is she from?” Kahoot countries quiz. HW: Write 3 sentences “I am from...”.
Monday mother, father, brother, sister, friend Family vocabulary via Quizlet. Teacher introduces “He is my brother.” Wordwall family tree drag & drop. Pair work: “Who is she? – She is my mother.” Kahoot family members. HW: Draw your family tree and label in English.
Tuesday tall, short, friendly, funny, quiet, shy, smart Adjectives with pictures and Quizlet. Teacher describes “He is tall.” Wordwall adjective sort. Students describe classmates: “She is friendly.” Kahoot adjectives. HW: Write 5 sentences describing family members.
Wednesday handsome, pretty, old, young, twenty, thirty, forty Introduce numbers 20‑100 using Quizlet audio. Teacher explains “twenty” = 20, “thirty” = 30, “forty” = 40, etc. Students repeat. Wordwall number match (digit ↔ word). Pairs ask “How old is he? – He is twenty.” Kahoot numbers and ages. HW: Write ages of 3 family members.
Thursday Review: all countries, family, adjectives, numbers 1‑100 (written forms) Review with Kahoot (full unit). Teacher leads conversation “He’s cute.” (p.19). Quizlet “Test” mode. Role‑play: “Who is that? – He is my brother. He is smart.” Final revision. HW: Prepare for Sunday’s test (focus on writing numbers 1‑100 as words).
Friday No class (official weekend)
Saturday No class (official weekend)
Next Sunday FINAL TEST – Unit 3 (1 hour)
Dictation (10 words: countries, family, adjectives, numbers), fill in blanks, matching, short writing, oral.

Weak performance plan: Extra Quizlet practice, peer tutoring, audio recordings, Class Dojo rewards.
