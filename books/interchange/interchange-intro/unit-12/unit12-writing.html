✍️ Unit 12: What's the matter? - Writing Task

📝 Describe a health problem and give advice

Instructions: Write a paragraph of 100-150 words. Choose one health problem (e.g., headache, cold, flu, stomachache). Include:

  • What the problem is (use "have + noun" or "feel + adjective")
  • What you or the person should do (use imperatives: take, rest, drink, don't...)
  • What you should avoid (don't...)
  • Where to go or who to see (doctor, dentist, etc.)
📌 Example:
"I have a terrible headache. My head hurts a lot. I feel awful. I think I should take some aspirin and rest in a dark room. I should not look at my phone or computer. I should drink water and avoid coffee. If the headache doesn't go away, I should see a doctor. I hope I feel better soon."
