Lesson Plan - Unit 10 (5 lectures + test)

Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour each). Test: Next Sunday (1 hour). No classes on Friday/Saturday.

Digital tools: Quizlet sets (vocabulary), Kahoot (review games), Wordwall (interactive activities).

DayDictation WordsWarm-up / Guided PracticeIndependent Practice (Digital & Pair)Wrap-up & HW
Sunday soccer, baseball, basketball, volleyball, tennis, golf Sports vocabulary with Quizlet flashcards. Teacher asks “What sport uses a ball?” Wordwall matching (sport ↔ picture). Students use Quizlet “Learn”. Pair work: “What sports do you like? – I like soccer.” Kahoot sports quiz. HW: Write 5 sentences “I like/don’t like ...”.
Monday hockey, swimming, running, cycling, skiing, yoga More sports with Quizlet. Teacher explains “You need ice for hockey.” Wordwall “sport equipment” match. Pair work: “Can you swim? – Yes, I can.” Kahoot sports & abilities. HW: Write 5 sentences using “can/can’t”.
Tuesday piano, singing, dancing, cooking, can, can't Learn other hobbies and ability expressions using Quizlet. Students repeat and make sentences. Wordwall “can/can’t” sentence builder. Students interview each other: “Can you play the piano?” Kahoot can/can’t. HW: Write 5 things you can do and 5 you can’t do.
Wednesday always, usually, often, sometimes, hardly ever, never Learn adverbs of frequency. Teacher shows percentages, students repeat. Explain 'hardly ever' = almost never. Wordwall “adverbs of frequency” ordering. Students talk about habits: “I sometimes play tennis.” Kahoot adverbs. HW: Write 5 sentences about how often you do activities.
Thursday Review all sports, abilities, adverbs Review with Kahoot (full unit). Teacher leads conversation “I love sports” (p.??). Quizlet “Test” mode. Role‑play: “What sports do you like? – I like soccer, but I can’t play it well.” Final revision. HW: Prepare for Sunday’s test (sports vocabulary, can/can’t, adverbs of frequency).
Friday No class (official weekend)
Saturday No class (official weekend)
Next Sunday FINAL TEST – Unit 10 (1 hour)
Dictation (10 sports/activities words), fill in blanks (can/can’t, adverbs), matching (sport ↔ equipment), short writing (describe your free‑time activities), oral (ask & answer about abilities and habits).

Weak performance plan: Extra Quizlet practice, peer tutoring, audio recordings, Class Dojo rewards.

Syllabus - Level 2A (Units 9-12)

Duration: 4 weeks (4 units) + final test
Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour/day), test on the next Sunday.
Audience: Adult beginners (Dyslexia-friendly)

Assessment: Dictation (10%), Quiz (20%), Participation (10%), Homework (10%), Presentations (10%), Unit tests (40%)

Dyslexia support: OpenDyslexic font, audio support, extra time, multi‑sensory activities (Quizlet, Wordwall, Kahoot).
