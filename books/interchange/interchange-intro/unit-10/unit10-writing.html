✍️ Unit 10: What sports do you play? - Writing Task

📝 Describe your favorite sports or free-time activities

Instructions: Write a paragraph of 100-150 words. Include:

  • What sports or activities you like (play or watch)
  • How often you do them (adverbs of frequency: always, usually, often, sometimes, hardly ever, never)
  • Who you do them with
  • Where you do them
  • Use "can" to talk about abilities (e.g., I can swim, but I can't play hockey)
📌 Example:
"I love sports, but I don't play many. I usually play soccer with my friends on Saturdays. We play at the park near my house. I can play soccer well, but I can't play basketball. I also like watching baseball on TV. Sometimes I go swimming in the summer. I hardly ever play golf because it's expensive. My brother can play the piano, but he doesn't like sports. What about you?"
