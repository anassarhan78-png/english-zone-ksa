Lesson Plan - Unit 1 (5 lectures + test)

Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour each). Test: Next Sunday (1 hour). No classes on Friday/Saturday.

Digital tools: Quizlet sets (vocabulary), Kahoot (review games), Wordwall (interactive activities).

DayDictation WordsWarm-up / Guided PracticeIndependent Practice (Digital & Pair)Wrap-up & HW
Sunday name, first, last, I, am, nice, meet, you Greetings & self-intro (teacher model). Use Wordwall matching game (name ↔ person). Students use Quizlet “Learn” mode for vocabulary. In pairs: “What’s your name? My name is...” Kahoot review (basic questions). HW: Write 3 sentences “My name is... I am a student.”
Monday Miss, Mr., Mrs., Ms., teacher, student, he, she Introduce titles with pictures. Use Wordwall group sort (male/female/married). Quizlet flashcards + live game. Pair work: “Who is she? – She is Ms. Chen.” Kahoot quiz on titles. HW: Write 4 sentences using different titles.
Tuesday book, class, math, phone, number, one, two, three Numbers 0‑10 via Quizlet (audio). Teacher says numbers, students repeat. Wordwall “number match” activity. Pairs ask “What’s your phone number?” Kahoot numbers game. HW: Write your phone number and a friend’s.
Wednesday my, your, his, her, our, this, what, is Possessive adjectives using Wordwall drag & drop. Teacher models “my book / your pen”. Students use Quizlet “Write” mode. In groups: “What’s this? – It’s his notebook.” Kahoot possessive adjectives. HW: Describe 3 objects in the classroom using “my/your/his/her”.
Thursday yes, no, not, in, and, there, too, are Review all vocabulary with Kahoot (unit review). Teacher leads conversation “He’s over there”. Quizlet “Test” mode. Pairs role‑play dialogue from page 5 (Excuse me, are you Steven?). Final revision: Wordwall “group sort” (positive/negative). HW: Prepare for Sunday’s test.
Friday No class (official weekend)
Saturday No class (official weekend)
Next Sunday FINAL TEST – Unit 1 (1 hour)
Dictation (10 words), fill in blanks, matching, short writing, oral (optional).

Weak performance plan: Extra Quizlet practice, peer tutoring, audio recordings, Class Dojo rewards.
