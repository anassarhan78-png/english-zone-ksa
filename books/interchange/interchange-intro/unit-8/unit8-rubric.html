Lesson Plan - Unit 8 (5 lectures + test)

Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour each). Test: Next Sunday (1 hour). No classes on Friday/Saturday.

Digital tools: Quizlet sets (vocabulary), Kahoot (review games), Wordwall (interactive activities).

DayDictation WordsWarm-up / Guided PracticeIndependent Practice (Digital & Pair)Wrap-up & HW
Sunday doctor, nurse, teacher, police officer, firefighter, chef, pilot Jobs vocabulary with Quizlet flashcards. Teacher asks “What does a doctor do?” Wordwall matching (job ↔ picture). Students use Quizlet “Learn”. Pair work: “What do you do? – I’m a teacher.” Kahoot jobs quiz. HW: Write 5 sentences “A doctor works in a hospital.”
Monday waiter, cashier, manager, receptionist, security guard, salesperson More jobs with Quizlet. Teacher explains “A waiter serves food.” Wordwall “job duties” match. Pair work: “What does a cashier do? – Handles money.” Kahoot jobs & duties. HW: Write 5 sentences describing what 5 different workers do.
Tuesday accountant, lawyer, judge, hospital, office, school, restaurant Introduce workplaces vocabulary using Quizlet. Students repeat. Wordwall “where do they work?” sorting. Students ask each other “Where does a lawyer work? – In an office.” Kahoot workplaces. HW: Write 5 sentences “A ... works in a ...”.
Wednesday hotel, store, exciting, dangerous, stressful, boring, difficult, easy Adjectives for jobs via Quizlet. Teacher asks “Is a firefighter’s job exciting? – Yes, it is.” Wordwall adjective match. Students describe jobs: “Being a pilot is exciting.” Kahoot job adjectives. HW: Write 5 sentences using adjectives to describe jobs.
Thursday interesting, relaxing, review all jobs, workplaces, adjectives Review with Kahoot (full unit). Teacher leads conversation “Please be careful!” (p.??). Quizlet “Test” mode. Role‑play: job interview – “What do you do? – I’m a manager. I work in a hotel.” Final revision. HW: Prepare for Sunday’s test (simple present questions, job vocabulary, adjectives).
Friday No class (official weekend)
Saturday No class (official weekend)
Next Sunday FINAL TEST – Unit 8 (1 hour)
Dictation (10 job words), fill in blanks (simple present questions), matching (job ↔ workplace), short writing (describe a job), oral (ask & answer about job).

Weak performance plan: Extra Quizlet practice, peer tutoring, audio recordings, Class Dojo rewards.

Syllabus - Level 1B (Units 5-8)

Duration: 4 weeks (4 units) + final test
Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour/day), test on the next Sunday.
Audience: Adult beginners (Dyslexia-friendly)

Assessment: Dictation (10%), Quiz (20%), Participation (10%), Homework (10%), Presentations (10%), Unit tests (40%)

Dyslexia support: OpenDyslexic font, audio support, extra time, multi‑sensory activities (Quizlet, Wordwall, Kahoot).
