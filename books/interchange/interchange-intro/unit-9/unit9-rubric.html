Lesson Plan - Unit 9 (5 lectures + test)

Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour each). Test: Next Sunday (1 hour). No classes on Friday/Saturday.

Digital tools: Quizlet sets (vocabulary), Kahoot (review games), Wordwall (interactive activities).

DayDictation WordsWarm-up / Guided PracticeIndependent Practice (Digital & Pair)Wrap-up & HW
Sunday apple, banana, bread, rice, chicken, fish Food vocabulary with Quizlet flashcards. Teacher shows pictures: “This is an apple.” Wordwall matching (food ↔ picture). Students use Quizlet “Learn”. Pair work: “Do you like apples? – Yes, I do.” Kahoot food quiz. HW: Write 5 sentences “I like/don’t like ...”.
Monday milk, cheese, egg, tomato, carrot, lettuce More foods and introduce count/noncount with Quizlet. Teacher explains “milk is noncount.” Wordwall “count vs noncount” sort. Pair work: “Do we need any eggs? – Yes, we need some eggs.” Kahoot count/noncount. HW: Write 5 sentences using “some” or “any”.
Tuesday coffee, tea, cake, cookie, always, usually 大家一起学习副词。教师使用 Quizlet 展示频率副词。学生跟读并造句。 Wordwall “adverbs of frequency” ordering. Students talk about habits: “I always drink coffee in the morning.” Kahoot adverbs. HW: Write 5 sentences about your habits using always/usually.
Wednesday often, sometimes, hardly ever, never More adverbs with Quizlet. Teacher asks “How often do you eat pizza?” Wordwall “frequency” match. Pair work: interview each other “How often do you...?” Kahoot frequency. HW: Write 5 questions with “How often do you...?” and answer them.
Thursday Review all foods and adverbs Review with Kahoot (full unit). Teacher leads conversation “How about some sandwiches?” (p.??). Quizlet “Test” mode. Role‑play: shopping for a picnic – “Do we have any bread? – No, we need some.” Final revision. HW: Prepare for Sunday’s test (count/noncount, some/any, adverbs of frequency).
Friday No class (official weekend)
Saturday No class (official weekend)
Next Sunday FINAL TEST – Unit 9 (1 hour)
Dictation (10 food words), fill in blanks (some/any, adverbs), matching (food ↔ category), short writing (describe your eating habits), oral (ask & answer about food preferences).

Weak performance plan: Extra Quizlet practice, peer tutoring, audio recordings, Class Dojo rewards.
