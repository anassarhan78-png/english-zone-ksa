Lesson Plan - Unit 2 (5 lectures + test)

Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour each). Test: Next Sunday (1 hour). No classes on Friday/Saturday.

Digital tools: Quizlet sets (vocabulary), Kahoot (review games), Wordwall (interactive activities).

DayDictation WordsWarm-up / Guided PracticeIndependent Practice (Digital & Pair)Wrap-up & HW
Sunday book, pen, pencil, eraser, notebook, desk Review classroom objects with Quizlet flashcards. Teacher models “This is a book.” Use Wordwall matching game. Students use Quizlet “Learn”. In pairs: “What’s this? – It’s a pen.” Kahoot vocabulary quiz. HW: Write 5 sentences “This is a/an ____.”
Monday chair, board, map, clock, window, door Introduce plurals with pictures. Wordwall “singular/plural” sort. Quizlet “Write” mode. Pair work: “What are these? – They are desks.” Kahoot plurals. HW: Write 5 plural sentences.
Tuesday bag, wallet, keys, umbrella, cell phone, sunglasses Articles a/an using Wordwall drag & drop. Teacher models “an umbrella”. Quizlet “Test” mode. Groups: “Is this a key? – Yes, it is.” Kahoot a/an. HW: List 5 things in your bag with a/an.
Wednesday camera, watch, prepositions: in, on, under, behind, next to Prepositions using Wordwall “where is it?” game. Teacher describes object positions. Students use Quizlet prepositions set. Pair work: “Where is the book? – It’s on the desk.” Kahoot prepositions. HW: Draw a room and write 5 preposition sentences.
Thursday Review all unit words Review with Kahoot (full unit). Teacher leads conversation “Oh, no! Where are my keys?” (p.11). Quizlet “Match” mode. Pairs role‑play the conversation. Final revision. HW: Prepare for Sunday’s test.
Friday No class (official weekend)
Saturday No class (official weekend)
Next Sunday FINAL TEST – Unit 2 (1 hour)
Dictation (10 words), fill in blanks, matching, short writing, oral (optional).

Weak performance plan: Extra Quizlet practice, peer tutoring, audio recordings, Class Dojo rewards.
