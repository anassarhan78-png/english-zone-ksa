Lesson Plan - Unit 9 (5 lectures + test)

Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour each). Test: Next Sunday (1 hour). No classes on Friday/Saturday.

Digital tools: Quizlet, Kahoot, Wordwall.

DayDictation WordsWarm-up / Guided PracticeIndependent Practice (Digital & Pair)Wrap-up & HW
Sunday long brown hair, short blond hair, straight black hair, curly red hair, bald, mustache and beard Hair vocabulary with Quizlet and pictures. Teacher models "She has long brown hair." Wordwall matching (hair type ↔ picture). Pair work: "What does he look like?" Kahoot hair quiz. HW: Describe your own hair in 2 sentences.
Monday young, middle-aged, elderly, short, fairly short, medium height, pretty tall, very tall Age and height vocabulary with Quizlet. Teacher asks "How old is she? How tall is he?" Wordwall "age & height" sorting. Pair work: ask and answer about age and height. Kahoot age & height. HW: Write 3 sentences about family members' age and height.
Tuesday handsome, good-looking, pretty, jeans, green shirt, sunglasses, hat Looks and clothing vocabulary. Teacher introduces modifiers: "the man wearing a green shirt". Wordwall drag & drop (modifiers). Students describe pictures: "She is the one with sunglasses." Kahoot looks & clothing. HW: Write 5 sentences using "wearing", "with", "in".
Wednesday neutral, vintage, outdated, alter, baggy, skinny Reading vocabulary from fashion advice column. Teacher explains each word with examples. Wordwall matching (word ↔ definition). Pair work: discuss what they like to wear. Kahoot reading vocab. HW: Read the advice column summary and answer 3 questions.
Thursday Review all words Review with Kahoot (full unit). Teacher leads conversation "Which one is she?" Quizlet "Test" mode. Role‑play: describe a classmate without saying their name. Final revision. HW: Prepare for Sunday's test (appearance, modifiers, reading vocabulary).
Friday No class (official weekend)
Saturday No class (official weekend)
Next Sunday FINAL TEST – Unit 9 (1 hour)
Dictation (10 words), fill in blanks (modifiers), matching (vocabulary), short writing (describe a person), oral (ask & answer about appearance).

Weak performance plan: Extra Quizlet practice, peer tutoring, audio recordings, Class Dojo rewards.

Syllabus - Interchange 1 (Level A2-B1)

Duration: 16 units + final review.
Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour/day). Test on the next Sunday.
Audience: Adult learners at CEFR A2-B1 level. Dyslexia-friendly font and activities.

Unit 9 topics: Describing people (hair, age, height, looks, clothing), modifiers (wearing/with/in/next to), fashion advice vocabulary (neutral, vintage, outdated, alter, baggy, skinny).

Assessment: Dictation (10%), Quiz (20%), Participation (10%), Homework (10%), Presentations (10%), Unit tests (40%)

Dyslexia support: OpenDyslexic font, audio support, extra time, multi‑sensory activities (Quizlet, Wordwall, Kahoot).
