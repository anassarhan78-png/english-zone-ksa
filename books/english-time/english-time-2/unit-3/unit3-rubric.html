💡 How parents can help / كيف يمكنك مساعدة طفلك:
- At a family gathering, ask "What is he doing?" "He is laughing."
- Practice the birthday dialogue before a real birthday.
- Play "I spy" with the /k/ sound (e.g., "I spy a cat").
- Use Quizlet live or Kahoot at home for fun revision.
- Praise effort, not just correct answers.
