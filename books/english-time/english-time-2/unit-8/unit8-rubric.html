💡 How parents can help / كيف يمكنك مساعدة طفلك:
- Ask "Where is the book?" and encourage using prepositions.
- Help your child clean up a room and use "Let's clean up" in English.
- Play "I spy" with long u words (e.g., "I spy something blue").
- Use Quizlet live or Kahoot at home for fun revision.
- Praise effort, not just correct answers.
