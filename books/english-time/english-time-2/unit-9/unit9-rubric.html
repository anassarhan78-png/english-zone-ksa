💡 How parents can help / كيف يمكنك مساعدة طفلك:
- At snack time, ask "Do you have candy?" "I have popcorn."
- Practice the conversation when you are in a hurry.
- Play "I spy" with short a and long a words (e.g., "I spy a cap" (short a), "I spy a game" (long a)).
- Use Quizlet live or Kahoot at home for fun revision.
- Praise effort, not just correct answers.
