💡 How parents can help / كيف يمكنك مساعدة طفلك:
- Ask "What are you doing?" at home and encourage "I am playing."
- Practice days of the week: "Today is Sunday."
- Play "I spy" with L and R sounds (e.g., "I spy a red rabbit").
- Use Quizlet live or Kahoot at home for fun revision.
- Praise effort, not just correct answers.

📚 English Time 2 – Course Syllabus (Level 2)

Duration: 10 weeks (10 units) + final review.
Schedule: Sunday – Thursday (1 hour/day). Test on the next Sunday.
Target: Young learners (ages 7-10). Dyslexia-friendly font and activities.

WeekUnitTopicsTest Date
1Unit 1Family members, How old are you?, phonics Jj/YyNext Sunday

Assessment / التقييم:
