### 🧩 Grammar: Near vs. Far (The Store Game)
> **How to Play:** The teacher holds an item (Near) or points to an item on a shelf (Far). Students write the full question and answer.

1. **What is this?** (Holding a pen 🖊️)
2. **It is a pen.**
3. **What is that?** (Pointing to a book far away 📚)
4. **It is a big book.**
5. **What is this? It is an eraser.** (Note: an + eraser!)

---
*💡 Interaction Tip: Have students "shop" from each other's desks. They point to a friend's ruler and ask: "What is that?"*
