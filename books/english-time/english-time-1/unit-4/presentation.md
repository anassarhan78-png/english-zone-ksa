# 🎤 Show & Tell: My Cool Stationery

**Topic:** Showing your school tools.
**Time:** ⏱️ 30-45 Seconds

### 📜 The Script (Action!)
1. **Intro:** "Hello! Welcome to my desk!"
2. **Near:** (Hold up your pencil) ➡️ "What is **this**? It is my yellow pencil."
3. **Far:** (Point to the teacher's desk) ➡️ "What is **that**? It is a big book."
4. **Closing:** "I love my school tools! Thank you! Bye!"

---
*⭐ Fun Rule: The presenter must use a ruler as a "pointer" during the presentation! 📏*
