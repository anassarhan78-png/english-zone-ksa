# Writing Activity: The Beautiful Meadow

### Task
Write 3 sentences about what you see in the meadow.

### Example Model
I am in the meadow. I see a yellow flower. It is a small bird.

### Student Worksheet
1. I see a __________. (tree / cloud)
2. It is a __________. (red / yellow) flower.
3. The __________ is in the sky. (sun / bird)
