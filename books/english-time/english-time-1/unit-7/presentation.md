# 🎬 The "Hurry Up!" Drama

**Topic:** A mother waiting for her daughter at the food court.
**Time:** ⏱️ 45 Seconds

### 📢 The Script (Roleplay!)
1. **Mother:** "Are you finished, [Name]?" (Look at your watch ⌚)
2. **Daughter:** "No, not yet. I like this spaghetti!" (Pretend to eat 🍝)
3. **Mother:** "Please hurry! We have school."
4. **Daughter:** "Okay, okay! I am finished now."
5. **Both:** "Thank you! Goodbye!"

---
*⭐ Fun Rule: The "Mother" must look very impatient, and the "Daughter" must act like she is eating the most delicious pizza ever!*
