# 🕵️ The Food Court Quiz

### ✅ Yes or No?
1. Do you like fish? 🐟 [ Yes ] / [ No ]
2. Are you finished with your lunch? 🍽️ [ Yes ] / [ No ]

### 🔍 Discovery Questions (Wh- Questions)
1. **What** do you like? (I like __________) 🍔
2. **What** does Mom say? (Please __________) 🏃‍♂️

### 🏗️ Sentence Builder (Fill in)
1. I ________ like salad. It is green. (like / am)
2. I ________ like fish. It is yucky. (don't / not)
3. Are you finished? No, not ________. (yet / yes)

---
### 🔑 Teacher Answer Key
* **Yes/No:** (Student's Choice)
* **Discovery:** 1. Burger / Pizza / etc. | 2. hurry
* **Build:** 1. like | 2. don't | 3. yet
