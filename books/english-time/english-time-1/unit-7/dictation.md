# 🍔 The Food Court Menu Dictation

### 🍕 Yummy Keywords
* **Burger** (🍔)
* **Spaghetti** (🍝)
* **Rice** (🍚)
* **Pizza** (🍕)
* **Salad** (🥗)
* **Fish** (🐟)

### 🕹️ How to Play: "The Hungry Waiter"
1. **The Order:** The teacher acts as a waiter and says "I want Pizza and Fish." Students must write **Pizza** and **Fish**.
2. **The Speed Test:** The teacher says "Hurry! Spell Burger!" Students race to write **Burger** before the teacher counts to 5.
3. **The Emoji Match:** Draw a 🥗 and write **Salad** next to it.

### 📝 Grammar: The "Hurry Up" Dialogue
1. **Are you finished?**
2. **No, not yet.**
3. **Please hurry!**
4. **I like pizza.**
5. **I don't like salad.**
