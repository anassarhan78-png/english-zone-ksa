# 🎤 Show & Tell: Meet the New Student

**Topic:** Introducing yourself and your favorite number.
**Time:** ⏱️ 30-45 Seconds

### 📢 The Script (Action!)
1. **Greeting:** "Hello everyone! Nice to meet you!" (Big smile & wave 👋)
2. **Identity:** "My **first name** is [Name]. My **last name** is [Name]."
3. **Numbers:** "I am [Age] years old. My favorite number is [1-12]!"
4. **Closing:** "Thank you! Goodbye!"

---
*⭐ Teacher's Trick: Let the student hold up a card with their favorite number on it during the talk!*
