# 🕵️ The School Data Quiz

### ✅ Number Quest (Write the word)
1. 1 + 1 = __________ (two)
2. 6 + 4 = __________ (ten)
3. 9 + 3 = __________ (twelve)

### 🔍 Discovery Questions (Wh- Questions)
1. **What** is your first name? ➡️ __________
2. **What** is your last name? ➡️ __________
3. **How** many students? ➡️ I see __________ (1-12).

### 🏗️ Sentence Builder (Fill in)
1. My ________ name is Ali. (first / one)
2. I am ________ years old. (twelve / teacher)
3. What is ________ name? (your / you)

---
### 🔑 Teacher's Answer Key
* **Quest:** 1. two | 2. ten | 3. twelve
* **Search:** (Student's names) | (Number of students)
* **Build:** 1. first | 2. twelve (or any number) | 3. your
