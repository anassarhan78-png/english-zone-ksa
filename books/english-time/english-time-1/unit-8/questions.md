# 🕵️ The Supermarket Mystery Quiz

### ✅ Yes or No?
1. Are carrots orange? 🥕 [ Yes ] / [ No ]
2. Do you like potatoes? 🥔 [ Yes ] / [ No ]

### 🔍 Discovery Questions (Wh- Questions)
1. **What** do you want to borrow? (A __________) 🖊️
2. **Do** you like bananas? (Yes, I ________) 🍌

### 🏗️ Sentence Builder (Fill in)
1. May I ________ a pen? (borrow / like)
2. Sure. ________ you are. (Here / There)
3. Do you like oranges? No, I ________. (don't / do)

---
### 🔑 Teacher Answer Key
* **Yes/No:** 1. Yes | 2. (Student's Choice)
* **Discovery:** 1. pen / pencil | 2. do
* **Build:** 1. borrow | 2. Here | 3. don't
