# 🎬 The "Polite Customer" Drama

**Topic:** Borrowing a pen at the supermarket checkout.
**Time:** ⏱️ 45 Seconds

### 📢 The Script (Roleplay!)
1. **Customer:** "Oh! I need to write. May I borrow a pen?" (Look around 🖊️)
2. **Shopkeeper:** "Sure. Here you are." (Hand over the pen)
3. **Customer:** "Thank you! Do you like oranges?" (Point to the fruit 🍊)
4. **Shopkeeper:** "Yes, I do. They are yummy!"
5. **Customer:** "I like oranges too. Goodbye!"
6. **Shopkeeper:** "Have a nice day! Bye!"

---
*⭐ Fun Rule: The "Customer" must say "Thank you" with a big, polite smile!*
