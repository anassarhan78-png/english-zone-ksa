# 🛒 The Supermarket Basket Dictation

### 🍎 Fresh Keywords
* **Apples** (🍎)
* **Oranges** (🍊)
* **Bananas** (🍌)
* **Cucumbers** (🥒)
* **Potatoes** (🥔)
* **Carrots** (🥕)

### 🕹️ How to Play: "The Shopping List"
1. **The Secret List:** The teacher whispers 3 items to a "Customer." The customer tells the "Shopkeeper." All students must write the 3 items they hear!
2. **Plural Power:** The teacher says "Apple." Students must write the plural form: **Apples**.
3. **The Color Match:** Draw a 🍊 and write **Oranges** next to it.

### 📝 Grammar: The Polite Request
1. **May I borrow a pen?**
2. **Sure. Here you are.**
3. **Thank you!**
4. **Do you like apples?** Yes, I do.
5. **Do you like carrots?** No, I don't.
