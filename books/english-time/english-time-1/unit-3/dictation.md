### 🧩 Grammar: Near vs. Far
> **The Finger Game:** If the teacher points to a picture on the desk, write "This". If the teacher points to the board, write "That".

1. **What is this?** (Near 📍)
2. **It is a small cat.**
3. **What is that?** (Far ☁️)
4. **It is a big horse.**
5. **What is that? It is a goat.**

---
*💡 Teacher Tip: Use a laser pointer or a stick to show "That" (Far) and touch the student's book for "This" (Near)!*
