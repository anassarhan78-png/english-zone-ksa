# 🕵️ The Far & Near Quiz

### ✅ Circle the Correct Word
1. (Pointing to something far) ➡️ What is (**this / that**)?
2. (Touching something on the desk) ➡️ What is (**this / that**)?

### 🔍 Search & Answer (Wh- Questions)
1. **What** is this? (It is a __________) 🐄
2. **What** is that? (It is a __________) 🐎

### 🏗️ Sentence Builder (Fill in)
1. What ________ this? (is / are)
2. ________ is a small cat. (It / You)
3. What is that? It is ________ goat. (a / an)

---
### 🔑 Teacher Answer Key
* **Circle:** 1. that | 2. this
* **Search:** 1. cow | 2. horse
* **Build:** 1. is | 2. It | 3. a
