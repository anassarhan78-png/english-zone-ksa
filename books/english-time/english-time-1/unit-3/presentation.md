# 🎤 Show & Tell: The Farm Tour Guide

**Topic:** Asking and answering about animals.
**Time:** ⏱️ 30-45 Seconds

### 📜 The Script (Action!)
1. **Hello:** "Hi! Welcome to my farm tour!"
2. **Near Check:** (Touch your drawing) ➡️ "What is **this**? It is a chicken!"
3. **Far Check:** (Point to a friend's drawing) ➡️ "What is **that**? It is a big cow!"
4. **Bye:** "Thank you for visiting! Goodbye!"

---
*⭐ Activity: Let students work in pairs. One points, the other asks "What is that?"*
