# 🎤 Show & Tell: The Happy Neighbor

**Topic:** Introducing yourself to the class.
**Time:** ⏱️ 30-45 Seconds

### 📢 The Script (Action!)
1. **Startup:** "Hello neighbors! Hi everyone!" (Wave your hand 👋)
2. **The Data:** "I am a [boy/girl]. My name is [Name]."
3. **The Logic:** "This is my neighborhood. You are my friend!"
4. **Shutdown:** "Thank you! Goodbye!" (Big bow 🎭)

---
*⭐ Activity: Wear a funny hat or glasses during the presentation to be a "cool neighbor"!*
