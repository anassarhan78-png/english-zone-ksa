# Presentation: Hello Neighbor!

**Topic:** Introducing yourself to a neighbor.
**Time:** 30-60 Seconds

### Structure
1.  **Greeting:** "Hello, everyone!"
2.  **Introduction:** "I am a [boy/girl]. My name is [Name]."
3.  **Connection:** "We are in the neighborhood. You are my friend."
4.  **Closing:** "Thank you! Goodbye!"
