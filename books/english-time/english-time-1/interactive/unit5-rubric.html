💡 How parents can help / كيف يمكنك مساعدة طفلك:
- Count objects around the house in English (apples, spoons, toys).
- Ask "How many?" questions during daily routines.
- Practice first and last name in English.
- Use Quizlet live or Kahoot at home for fun revision.
- Praise effort, not just correct answers.
