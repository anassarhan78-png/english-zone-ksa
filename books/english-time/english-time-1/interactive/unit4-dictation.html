✏️ Unit 4: Stationery Store 🎒

🎮 How to Play "The Backpack Search":

  • 1️⃣ The Grab: Teacher asks for an item (e.g., ruler). First to hold it up and write "ruler" wins.
  • 2️⃣ The Whisper: Teacher whispers "paper". Students say "Pardon me?" then write it with 'a'.
  • 3️⃣ The Speed Draw: Draw 🧼 and write "eraser" next to it in 5 seconds!

📘 Grammar: What is this / that?

✔ What is this? (near) → It is an eraser.
✔ What is that? (far) → It is a ruler.
✔ May I borrow a pen?
