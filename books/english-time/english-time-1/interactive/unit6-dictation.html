🤸 Unit 6: In Gym Class (Feelings) 😊

🎮 How to Play "The Emotion Mirror":

  • 1️⃣ The Emoji Face: Look at the emoji and draw the feeling.
  • 2️⃣ The Ouch Test: Teacher says "Ouch!". You write "Are you okay?".
  • 3️⃣ Phonetic Check: Always use ɑ in words like "Hɑppy" and "Sɑd".

📘 Grammar: Are you...?

✔ Are you okay? → I think so.
✔ I ɑm hɑppy. / I ɑm not sɑd.
✔ Are you hungry? → Yes, I ɑm. / No, I ɑm not.
