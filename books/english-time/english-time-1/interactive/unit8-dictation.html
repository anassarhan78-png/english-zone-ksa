🎮 How to Play "The Grocery List":

  • 1️⃣ The Cart: Draw the fruit or vegetable in the shopping bag.
  • 2️⃣ The Question: Answer: "Do you like ɑpples?" → "Yes, I do."
  • 3️⃣ Phonetic Check: Use ɑ in "ɑpples", "Bɑnɑnɑs", and "Cɑrrots".

📘 Grammar: Questions with Do

✔ Do you like oranges? → Yes, I do.
✔ Do you like cucumbers? → No, I don't.
✔ Mɑy I borrow ɑ pen? → Sure. Here you ɑre.
