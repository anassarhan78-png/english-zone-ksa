💡 How parents can help / كيف يمكنك مساعدة طفلك:
- Point to community helpers in your town and say the job in English.
- Practice telephone numbers in English (your home number or a pretend number).
- Play "short i" word games (e.g., "I spy something that rhymes with 'sit'").
- Use Quizlet live or Kahoot at home for fun revision.
- Praise effort, not just correct answers.
