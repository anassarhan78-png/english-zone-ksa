🌿 Unit 2: Meadow Discovery 🦋

🎮 How to Play "The Distance Race":

  • 1️⃣ The Touch (This): Teacher touches a nearby picture and asks "What is this?" → You write the word.
  • 2️⃣ The Point (That): Teacher points to something far (like a cloud) and asks "What is that?" → You write the word.
  • 3️⃣ The Butterfly Flight: Draw a butterfly 🦋 and write "Butterfly" next to it quickly!

📘 Grammar: This / That

✔ What is this? (near) → It is a flower.
✔ What is that? (far) → It is a cloud.
✔ That is a bird in the tree.
