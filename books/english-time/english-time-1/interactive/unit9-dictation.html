🎪 Unit 9: At the Circus 🤡

🎮 How to Play "The Circus Show":

  • 1️⃣ The Artist: Draw a tɑll or short person in the circus ring.
  • 2️⃣ The Description: Answer: "Is she tɑll?" → "Yes, she is."
  • 3️⃣ Phonetic Check: Use ɑ in "Tɑll", "Smɑll", and "Fɑt".

📘 Grammar: Describing People

✔ He is tɑll. / She is short.
✔ Is he old? → Yes, he is.
✔ Is she young? → No, she isn't.
