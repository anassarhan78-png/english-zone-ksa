💡 How parents can help / كيف يمكنك مساعدة طفلك:
- Describe people you see (e.g., "That man is tall.").
- Play "I spy" with short e words (e.g., "I spy a pen").
- Ask "What's wrong?" when your child seems upset, practice the dialogue.
- Use Quizlet live or Kahoot at home for fun revision.
- Praise effort, not just correct answers.
