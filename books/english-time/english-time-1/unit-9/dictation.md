# 🤡 The Circus Opposites Dictation

### 🎪 Circus Keywords (Opposites)
* **Tall** (🦒) / **Short** (🐒)
* **Thin** (🦯) / **Fat** (🎈)
* **Young** (👶) / **Old** (👴)

### 🕹️ How to Play: "The Human Statue"
1. **The Giant:** Teacher says "T-A-L-L". Students must stand on their tiptoes and reach high while writing **Tall**.
2. **The Mouse:** Teacher says "S-H-O-R-T". Students must crouch down low while writing **Short**.
3. **The Balloon:** Teacher says "F-A-T". Students puff out their cheeks and write **Fat**.

### 📝 Grammar: Describing People
1. **What is wrong?**
2. **I feel sick.**
3. **That is too bad.**
4. **He is thin. He isn't fat.**
5. **She is young. She isn't old.**
