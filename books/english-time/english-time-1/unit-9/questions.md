# 🕵️ The Circus Mystery Quiz

### ✅ Choose the Opposite
1. He is not tall. He is __________. (short / fat)
2. She is not old. She is __________. (young / thin)

### 🔍 Discovery Questions (Wh- Questions)
1. **What** is wrong? (I feel __________) 🤢
2. **Is** he fat? (No, he __________) 🦯

### 🏗️ Sentence Builder (Fill in)
1. That ________ too bad. (is / are)
2. She is young. She ________ old. (isn't / is)
3. I feel ________. (sick / happy)

---
### 🔑 Teacher Answer Key
* **Choose:** 1. short | 2. young
* **Discovery:** 1. sick | 2. isn't (or "is thin")
* **Build:** 1. is | 2. isn't | 3. sick
