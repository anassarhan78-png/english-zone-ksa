# 🎬 The "Sick Clown" Drama

**Topic:** Checking on a circus performer who doesn't feel well.
**Time:** ⏱️ 45 Seconds

### 📢 The Script (Roleplay!)
1. **Ringmaster:** "What is wrong, Mr. Clown?" (Look worried 🎩)
2. **Clown:** "I feel sick." (Hold your tummy 🤢)
3. **Ringmaster:** "That is too bad! Look at him. He is **thin**."
4. **Clown:** "Yes, I am not **fat** today. I need to rest."
5. **Ringmaster:** "Get well soon! Goodbye!"
6. **Clown:** "Thank you. Goodbye!"

---
*⭐ Fun Rule: The "Clown" must act very weak and tired, while the "Ringmaster" uses a very loud circus voice!*
