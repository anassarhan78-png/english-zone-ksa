# 📝 The Circus Talent Scout

### 🖍️ Task: "The Circus Poster"
*Draw two performers at the circus. One is tall, one is short. Describe them!*

---
| 🖼️ Draw the Performers | 🖋️ My Circus Report |
| :--- | :--- |
|           | 1. He is __________. (tall / thin) |
|   [DRAW]  | 2. He isn't __________. (short / fat) |
|           | 3. What is wrong? I __________ __________. |
---

### 💡 Student Model:
"Look at the clown! He is fat. He isn't thin. Oh no! What is wrong? I feel sick. That is too bad."
