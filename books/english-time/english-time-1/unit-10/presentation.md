# 🎬 The "Pardon Me" Phone Call

**Topic:** Asking for help and verifying a phone number.
**Time:** ⏱️ 45 Seconds

### 📢 The Script (Action!)
1. **A:** "Hello! What is your telephone number?" (Hold a phone 📱)
2. **B:** "It is 056154875."
3. **A:** "Pardon me?" (Lean in closer 👂)
4. **B:** "056154875!" (Speak slowly and clearly)
5. **A:** "Thank you. Is she a nurse?" (Point to a picture 💊)
6. **B:** "Yes, she is. Goodbye!"

---
*⭐ Fun Rule: Students must act like the phone line is "static" or "noisy" so they have a reason to say "Pardon me?"*
