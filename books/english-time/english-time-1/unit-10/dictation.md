# ✍️ The "Around Town" Heroes Dictation

### 👮 Community Helpers (Keywords)
* **Doctor** (🩺)
* **Nurse** (💊)
* **Firefighter** (👨‍🚒)
* **Mail Carrier** (📬)
* **Police Officer** (🚔)
* **Teacher** (👨‍🏫)

### 🕹️ How to Play: "The Siren & The Whisper"
1. **The Emergency:** Teacher makes a "Wooo-Wooo" siren sound. Students must write **Police Officer** or **Firefighter**.
2. **The Pardon Game:** Teacher whispers a phone number very quietly. Students must say "Pardon me?" and the teacher repeats it slowly.
3. **The Letter Hunt:** Draw a small ✉️ and write **Mail Carrier** next to it.

### 📝 Grammar: Checking the Facts
1. **What is your telephone number?**
2. **It is 056154875.**
3. **Pardon me?** (It is 056154875.)
4. **Is he a doctor?** Yes, he is.
5. **Is she a nurse?** No, she isn't.
