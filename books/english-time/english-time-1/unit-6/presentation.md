# 🎤 Show & Tell: The "Ouch" Drama

**Topic:** Acting out a small accident and checking on a friend.
**Time:** ⏱️ 30-45 Seconds

### 📢 The Script (Action!)
1. **The Trip:** (Pretend to fall) ➡️ "Ouch!"
2. **The Question:** "Are you okay?"
3. **The Answer:** "I think so. But I am **thirsty**!"
4. **The Check:** "Are you **happy** now?"
5. **The Final Answer:** "Yes, I am. Thank you! Goodbye!"

---
*⭐ Fun Rule: The presenter must use a real "sad" voice for the Ouch and a "happy" voice for the ending!*
