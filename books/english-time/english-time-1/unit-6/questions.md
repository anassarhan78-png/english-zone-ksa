# 🕵️ The Feelings Mystery Quiz

### ✅ Yes or No?
1. Are you hot in the sun? ☀️ [ Yes ] / [ No ]
2. Are you hungry for pizza? 🍕 [ Yes ] / [ No ]

### 🔍 Search & Answer (Wh- Questions)
1. **How** are you? (I am __________) 😊
2. **Are** you okay? (I __________ so.)

### 🏗️ Sentence Builder (Fill in)
1. Are you happy? Yes, I ________. (am / is)
2. Are you sad? No, I am ________. (not / no)
3. I am ________. Give me water. (thirsty / hungry)

---
### 🔑 Teacher Answer Key
* **Yes/No:** 1. Yes | 2. Yes
* **Search:** 1. happy / sad / etc. | 2. think
* **Build:** 1. am | 2. not | 3. thirsty
