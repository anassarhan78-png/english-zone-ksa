# 🎭 The Feelings Emoji Game: Unit 6

### 😊 Mood Keywords
* **Happy** (😊)
* **Sad** (😢)
* **Hot** (🥵)
* **Cold** (🥶)
* **Hungry** (🍕)
* **Thirsty** (🥤)

### 🕹️ How to Play: "The Emoji Race"
1. **The Mirror:** Teacher makes a "Sad" face. Students mirror it and shout "S-A-D" then write it!
2. **The Weather:** Teacher pretends to be "Hot" (fanning). Students write **Hot** and draw a tiny sun.
3. **The Ice Man:** Teacher shivers (Cold). Students write **Cold** and draw a snowflake.

### 📝 Grammar: The "Ouch" Dialogue
1. **Ouch!**
2. **Are you okay?**
3. **I think so.**
4. **Are you happy?** Yes, I am.
5. **Are you thirsty?** No, I am not.
